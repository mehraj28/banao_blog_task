  2. Go to settings
  3. Select your calendar from the left-hand menu.
  4. Access Calendar Settings by clicking on the three vertical dots next to the calendar name and selecting "Settings and sharing."
  5. Scroll down to the "Share with specific people" section and click on "Add people."
  6. Enter the email {{ service_account_email }} in the email field.
  7. From the permissions dropdown, select "Make changes to events" and click "Send."
